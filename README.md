# FortifAI

**FortifAI** is an AI-powered cybersecurity training platform designed for small and medium-sized businesses (SMBs). With 95% of breaches stemming from human error, FortifAI addresses the human side of cybersecurity through adaptive, role-specific training that evolves with the threat landscape.

## Overview

FortifAI is positioned in the global cybersecurity training market (valued at $5.23B in 2024, projected to grow to $13.70B by 2030 at 17.4% CAGR). The platform targets U.S. SMBs — a $386.5M serviceable obtainable market — with AI-driven personalization.

Key proposal components:

- **Problem**: 43% of cyberattacks target SMBs; 60% shut down within 6 months of a breach; 95% of incidents trace back to human error.
- **Solution**: Adaptive, role-specific AI training that updates with evolving threats.
- **Revenue model**: SaaS subscription with tiered pricing per employee.
- **Valuation**: Pre-money valuation of $7.5M based on multiple methods (DCF, comparables, precedent transactions).
- **Funding ask**: $3.8M (equity + convertible debt + debt).
